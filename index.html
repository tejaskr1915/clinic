Dr. H.M. Prema, BDS

Consultant Dental Surgeon

Dr. H.M. Prema is an experienced dental surgeon dedicated to providing exceptional oral healthcare. With extensive expertise in preventive, restorative and cosmetic dentistry, she believes in patient-first approach and evidence-based treatment methods.

Specializing in modern dental techniques, she ensures painless procedures while maintaining the highest standards of sterilization and clinical excellence. Her gentle approach and commitment to patient comfort have made her a trusted dentist in the Kundalahalli area.

She continuously updates her knowledge with the latest advancements in dentistry to provide the best possible care for all her patients.
